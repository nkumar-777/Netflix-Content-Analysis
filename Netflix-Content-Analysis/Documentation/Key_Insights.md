# Key Business Insights

## Project
**Netflix Content Analysis**

## Overview

After cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing the Netflix dataset, several meaningful business insights were identified. These insights help understand Netflix's content strategy, global distribution, and audience focus.

---

# Key Insights

## 1. Movies Dominate the Netflix Catalog

- Netflix offers **8,807** titles in total.
- **Movies account for 6,131 titles (approximately 69.6%)**.
- **TV Shows account for 2,676 titles (approximately 30.4%)**.

### Business Insight

Netflix's library is heavily focused on movies, indicating a stronger investment in movie content than television series.

---

## 2. United States Contributes the Most Content

The **United States** has the highest number of titles available on Netflix, followed by countries such as **India** and the **United Kingdom**.

### Business Insight

Netflix relies heavily on content produced in the United States while expanding its catalog with international productions.

---

## 3. Content Growth Increased Rapidly After 2015

The number of titles released on Netflix increased significantly after **2015**, reaching its highest levels during the late 2010s.

### Business Insight

This reflects Netflix's rapid global expansion and increased investment in original and licensed content during this period.

---

## 4. Mature Audience Content is Most Common

Content ratings such as **TV-MA** and **TV-14** appear most frequently in the dataset.

### Business Insight

Netflix primarily targets adult and teenage audiences by offering a large collection of mature and general entertainment content.

---

## 5. Drama and International Content are Highly Popular

Drama-related and international genres appear among the most common categories in the Netflix library.

### Business Insight

Netflix emphasizes diverse storytelling and international programming to attract a global audience.

---

## 6. Interactive Dashboard Improves Analysis

The dashboard allows users to filter data by:

- Country
- Release Year
- Content Type

### Business Insight

Interactive filtering enables stakeholders to explore trends quickly and make data-driven decisions without manually processing the dataset.

---

# Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can be considered:

- Continue investing in successful movie content while expanding high-quality TV series.
- Increase production and licensing in emerging international markets.
- Focus on high-performing genres to maximize audience engagement.
- Monitor yearly content growth to optimize future content acquisition strategies.
