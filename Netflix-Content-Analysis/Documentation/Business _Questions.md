# Business Questions

## Project
**Netflix Content Analysis**

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze Netflix's content library and uncover meaningful insights that can help stakeholders understand content distribution, trends, audience targeting, and global content availability.

---


# Expected Business Outcomes

This analysis helps answer important business questions such as:

- What type of content dominates Netflix?
- Which countries contribute the largest share of content?
- How has Netflix's catalog evolved over time?
- Which audience ratings are most common?
- Which genres are most frequently available?
- How does content distribution change using interactive filters?

---

# Stakeholders

This dashboard can be useful for:

- Business Analysts
- Data Analysts
- Content Strategy Teams
- Marketing Teams
- Decision Makers

---

# Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- SQL (MySQL Workbench)
- Power Query
- Power BI

---

# Project Outcome

The final dashboard transforms raw Netflix data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, SQL analysis, interactive visualizations, and KPI reporting, enabling users to explore Netflix's content library efficiently.
