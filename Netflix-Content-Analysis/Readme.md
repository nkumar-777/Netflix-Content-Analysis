# рџ“€ Key Insights

- Netflix hosts **8,807** titles.
- Movies account for approximately **69.6%** of the catalog.
- TV Shows account for approximately **30.4%**.
- The **United States** contributes the largest number of titles.
- Netflix experienced significant content growth after **2015**.
- **TV-MA** is the most common content rating.
- Drama and International content dominate the platform.
